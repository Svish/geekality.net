1. Hit <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>C</kbd> to toggle the console
1. Enter `testingcheats true`
1. Enter `cas.fulleditmode`
1. <kbd>Shift</kbd>-click your character and select "Modify in CAS"
