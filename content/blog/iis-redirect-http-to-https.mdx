To cleanly redirect HTTP to HTTPS with IIS, first install the [URL Rewrite module](http://www.iis.net/downloads/microsoft/url-rewrite), and then add the following to your `web.config`. Also remember to have bindings defined for your site for both HTTPS(443) _and_ HTTP(80).

```xml
<configuration>
  <system.webServer>
    <rewrite>
      <rules>
        <rule name="HTTP to HTTPS redirect" stopProcessing="true">
          <match url="(.*)" />
          <conditions>
            <add input="{HTTPS}" pattern="off" ignoreCase="true" />
          </conditions>
          <action
            type="Redirect"
            redirectType="Permanent"
            url="https://{HTTP_HOST}/{R:1}"
          />
        </rule>
      </rules>
    </rewrite>
  </system.webServer>
</configuration>
```
